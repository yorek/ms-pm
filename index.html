Being a Program Manager at Microsoft

What is this website? I (Davide) have been asked thousand times what means to be a PM in Microsoft and exactly what a PM does. My friend Matthew Roche recorded what is in my opinion the best and most complete answer possible, and so I though it could be helpful to make that answer even more accessible and discoverable by anyone interested. Below here you'll find the annotaded and enhanced transcript of Matthew's recording. If you also want to contribute and improve the transcript or the look-and-feel, head to the GitHub Repo.
